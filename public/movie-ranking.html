The Best Movies

Jessie's top 3 movies of all-time

The Shawshank Redemption

Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins) is sentenced to two consecutive life terms in prison
for the murders of his wife and her lover and is sentenced to a tough prison.
However, only Andy knows he didn't commit the crimes.

Kabhi Kushi Khabi Ghum

Rahul (Shah Rukh Khan), the adoptive son of business magnate Yash Raichand
(Amitabh Bachchan), feels eternal gratitude to his father for rescuing him from a
life of poverty. Yet, when Yash forbids his love of poor Anjali (Kajol), Rahul marries
her and moves to London with new wife and sister-in-law, Pooja (Kareena Kapoor),
breaking the heart of his mother (Jaya Bachchan). Ten years later, Rahul's younger brother
(Hrithik Roshan) comes to London intent on brokering peace between father and son.

Veer

A bollywood favourite, Prithvi seeks revenge on Gyanendra Singh and the British.
He sends his son Veer to Britain to study their plans. Veer kills Gyanendra's son
but ends up falling in love with his daughter.
